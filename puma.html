A Brief History of Puma

2018: Adidas Stop Sponsoring Puma for Their Support of the IDF and for Operating in Forcibly Taken Israeli Settlements.

Adidas stopped sponsoring Puma in July 2018 due to Puma sponsoring the Israel Football Association (IFA), which has teams in and play in unjustly occupied Palestinian territories. This decision by Adidas came after an international campaign and receiving over 16,000 signatures asking for them to stop being complicit in Puma's actions.
